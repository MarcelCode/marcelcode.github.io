Overview

This website is designed with privacy in mind. This privacy policy explains how personal data is handled when you visit this website.

Key Points:

  • No cookies are used

Data Collection

Automatic Data Collection

When you visit this website, standard technical information may be temporarily stored in server log files by the hosting provider. This typically includes:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of access
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referrer URL (the page from which you accessed this site)

This data is collected for technical reasons to ensure the stability and security of the website. It is not combined with other data sources and is automatically deleted after a short period.

Contact via Email

If you contact me via email or through the contact form, the personal data you provide (name, email address, message content) will be stored solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. This data will not be shared with third parties and will be deleted once it is no longer needed for processing your request, unless legal retention requirements apply.

Third-Party Services

Google Fonts

This website uses Google Fonts to display fonts. When you access a page, your browser may load these fonts directly from Google's servers. Google may collect technical data (such as IP address) during this process. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

External Links

This website contains links to external websites (such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and my employer's website). I have no control over the data processing practices of these third-party sites. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information about how they handle your data.

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You can request information about the personal data stored about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Right to restriction: You can request restriction of processing under certain conditions.
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me at: marcel@siegmann.dev
